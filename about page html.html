Routine childhood vaccinations are an important way to ensure that your child and community remain healthy and protected against serious diseases.
If not done so, this can lead to many horrendous repercussions.
Hence, this is a website rather an online platform that guides, appoints and certifies child vaccinations.
...

WHY NVL?

NVL will assure that every newborn gets proper access to vaccinations in their actual present time location.
So, that more and more of the world’s children are vaccinated and protected.
Our website allows parents to login to our website by entering valid email id or their respective phone numbers,
resulting in an automated OTP generation, so that user login details can be authenticized.
The user will also get a standard chart of required vaccines and then you can search the nearest vaccination center to get the job done.
The center will then update the child’s vaccination chart.

AIM:

The main aim is to get 100% vaccinated across regions.
To display the total number of registrations with respect to state/area or nearest hospitals.
Addition of locations of the hospitals based on the data you input on the website.
To display the timings and availability of the doses in the selected hospitals.
